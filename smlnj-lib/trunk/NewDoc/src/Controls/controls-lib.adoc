For https://smlnj.org[*SML/NJ*], include `$/controls-lib.cm` in your
*CM* file.

For use in http://www.mlton.org/[*MLton*], include
`$(SML_LIB)/smlnj-lib/Controls/controls-lib.mlb` in your *MLB* file.
