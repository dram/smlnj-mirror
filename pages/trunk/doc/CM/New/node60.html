9. The pervasive environment

The pervasive environment can be thought of as a compilation unit that all compilation units implicitly depend upon. The pervasive enviroment exports all non-modular bindings (types, values, infix operators, overloaded symbols) that are mandated by the specification for the Standard ML Basis Library [RG99]. (All other bindings of the Basis Library are exported by $/basis.cm which is a genuine CM library.)

The pervasive environment is the only place where CM conveys non-modular bindings from one compilation unit to another, and its definition is fixed.
