Back End Optimizations

Once MLRisc trees have been generated, they are passed into a module that generates a flowgraph of target machine instructions. Again, this module and all subsequent optimization phases have been specialized to the front end. Nearly all instruction selection modules provided by MLRISC use a simple tree pattern matching algorithm rather than the more heavy weight BURG tools --- including the x86 It is important to emphasis that all optimizations are performed on the flowgraph of target machine instructions and not MLRisc instructions. There is complete flexibility in the order, and nature of the optimizations performed.
